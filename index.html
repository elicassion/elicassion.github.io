I'm Jinghuan Shang from China. Now I'm a CS Ph.D. student at Stony Brook University. Previously, I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, member of IEEE Honored Class major in Computer Science.

My research interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My ultimate research goal is to establish intelligent interfaces and systems improving human-computer cooperation. Boosting by applied machine learning, systems will replace repeating tasks with auto-completion with computing, which eases human from tedious routine works. Beyond my major, I love design and computer games. Therefore, I'm also fascinated by computer-assist and computer-generated arts.
